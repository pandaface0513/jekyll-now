Design Rational

We hope to have our users to play musician. Not everyone has the fortune to learn an instrument. Be it of a money issue or time issue. Our solution is hoping to give everyone a chance to compose a song, that represents himself/herself. All that without the expertise to play a guitar.

Technical Details

As for technical details, there would be one input and at least one kind of output.




Scroll down for more.

Input

Our input would be from the users' microphone.

                        
                            navigator.getUserMedia({audio:true});

Output(s)

Our ideal outputs would be both a graphic visualizer and a melody created from the input. However, with the time contraints, we might not be able to achieve both.

Logical Steps

  1. access to microphone
  2. receive the fft data and analyze it
  3. map the pitch to a particular piano/guitar note
  4. store the melody and then play it
  5. generate graphics according to the melody

Steps by steps interaction

  1. user starts the application by pressing a button
  2. the app will analyze the input in real time
  3. the resulted instrument notes be stored in an array
  4. give user a limit of 30 seconds
  5. after the recording, the app will play the stored melody
  6. and generate the graphics in real time
